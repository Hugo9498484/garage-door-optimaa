Off-Track Repair

Reset doors that have jumped their tracks after an impact.

Off-track doors are usually the result of a bumper tap or a broken cable. We inspect the tracks, rollers, and cables for hidden damage, then reset the door and re-tension the springs — often on the first visit.

How we handle off-track repair calls

  1. Same-day scheduling. Call before noon and we usually reach you the same day.
  2. On-site diagnostic. Written estimate before any work.
  3. Fixed-price repair. You approve up front.
  4. Post-repair test. Full cycle to confirm the fix.

Where we work

Available across our full coverage area: Alabama, Alaska, Arizona, Arkansas, California, Colorado, Connecticut, Delaware, District of Columbia, Florida, Georgia, Hawaii.
