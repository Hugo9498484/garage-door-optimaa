Torsion Spring Replacement

Same-day residential torsion spring replacement, in pairs.

Torsion springs are the highest-tension component in your garage. A broken spring turns a 200-pound door into a hazard the opener can't lift. We replace springs in pairs, sized to your exact door weight, with wind-count matched to travel — so both springs wear evenly and last their full cycle rating.

How we handle torsion spring replacement calls

  1. Same-day scheduling. Call before noon and we usually reach you the same day.
  2. On-site diagnostic. Written estimate before any work.
  3. Fixed-price repair. You approve up front.
  4. Post-repair test. Full cycle to confirm the fix.

Where we work

Available across our full coverage area: Alabama, Alaska, Arizona, Arkansas, California, Colorado, Connecticut, Delaware, District of Columbia, Florida, Georgia.
